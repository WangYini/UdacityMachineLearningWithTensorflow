P(A&B) = P(A|B)*P(B) = P(B|A)*P(A)

if A and B are independent, P(A&B) = P(A)*P(B)

Prior (what we know): P(A), P(B|A), P(B|~A)

(what we know naturally due to compliment): P(~A), P(~B|A), P(~B|~A)

Posterior (what we infer): P(A|B)

(other forms): P(A|~B), P(~A|B), P(~A|~B)


P(A|B) = P(B|A)*P(A)/P(B)
in which P(B) = P(B|A)*P(A) + P(B|~A)*P(~A)
